Master the use of tools

To get the best results when you are working with wood, it is essential to use the right tool at the right time. You will learn from handtools all the way up to stationary machines, such as planers and tablesaws and the respective safety measures to operate them. Here we offer several courses to teach you and give you all kind of tips to make your work shine.

Working sustainable

This is the most important part of our beliefs. Nature is the most precius gift of all, and we love it.
